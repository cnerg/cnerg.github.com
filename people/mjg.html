Graduate Appointment¶

Nuclear Engineering University Programs Fellow

Matthew assists research efforts in the development of CYCLUS, a nuclear fuel cycle simulations code. He is concentrating on questions related to near and far-term energy policy, specifically investigating relationship and contract related constraints on the fuel cycle’s supply and demand system.

Research Interests¶

  • Energy Policy Analysis Tools
  • Nuclear Fuel Cycle Simulations
  • Proliferation Pathways Analysis
  • Fuel Cycle Internationalization Analysis
  • Nuclear Nonproliferation Policy Analysis
  • Linear/Non-Linear Programming
